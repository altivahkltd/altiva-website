§ 05 About 2000 characters max · EN + FR

The full story, edited for LinkedIn.

LinkedIn truncates after ~300 characters, so the first paragraph must land the promise alone. The rest deepens it — positioning, practice, reach, then contact. English is primary (international audience); French is available to paste when you post bilingually.

About · English

Operator. Not consultant.

Altiva is an international advisory platform for situations where execution is the bottleneck. Not teams of analysts producing decks from a distance. One senior operator, embedded in the room — with real P&L experience, carrying judgment in, taking decisions, moving execution until the mandate is closed.

We are engaged when the strategy is sound and the growth is there, but the organisation is not moving. Interim COO mandates. High-stakes advisory to CEOs and executive committees. Transformation and AI programmes that are business-driven, not layered on top. Go-to-market build-out across EU and APAC. Operational reviews. Transaction preparation — fundraise, exit, repositioning.

Six mandate types, six geographies. Work already delivered across automation, Fortune 500 industrials, regional services businesses, channel programmes and enterprise operations — in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific.

Anchored in Hong Kong with deep expertise across Europe & Asia-Pacific. Written work and methodology on altiva.hk. Direct enquiries: julien@altiva.hk.
